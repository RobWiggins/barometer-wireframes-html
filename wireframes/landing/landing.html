The Barometer web application grants users the ability to quantitatively measure the general population’s emotional view of events and subjects in real-time. Through combining Twitter's tweet search result API and IBM’s natural language processing model, the results are aggregated into 6 defined emotional sentiment categories (%): sadness, joy, fear, disgust, anger, and overall positive vs. negative outlook. The application is effectively a continuous, user-search driven online social presence survey.
